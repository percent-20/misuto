FAQ

Common questions

What exactly does Misuto check on the employee's phone?+

Misuto uses multiple sensors and signals built into the employee's phone to read their physical environment. The combination creates a unique environmental signature that's extremely hard to fake. We don't disclose the specific signals for security reasons.

Does the employee need to install an app?+

Employees install a lightweight companion app once during onboarding. After that, there are no daily interactions. Most employees forget it's there.

What about working from home, cafés, or while traveling?+

Employees can register multiple trusted environments with schedules. Adding a new regular location takes seconds.

What if an employee loses their phone?+

IT revokes the lost device immediately. The employee re-enrolls on their new phone. No hardware to ship, no tokens to replace.

Does Misuto replace existing MFA?+

Yes. Misuto replaces traditional MFA entirely. No more push notifications, OTP codes, or hardware tokens. Eliminates identity tax and protects against phishing.

How long does deployment take?+

Typically 2–4 weeks. Start with a pilot group, then expand. No infrastructure to rebuild.

What about compliance (NIST, SOC 2, GDPR)?+

Misuto never stores raw sensor data. Only encrypted, irreversible proofs. Aligns with GDPR data minimization and SOC 2 controls.

What if Misuto's servers go down?+

By default, Misuto fails closed. For business-critical apps, you can configure a fallback to your existing MFA.
