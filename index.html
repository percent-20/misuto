The Solution

Meet Misuto: Dynamic Environmental Signatures

Your phone silently generates a cryptographic proof from its physical context. Continuously changing. Impossible to replay. Completely frictionless.

1

Device collects context silently

WiFi networks, GPS coordinates, time of day, nearby Bluetooth devices. All captured in the background without any user action.

2

Hashed on-device into a short-lived proof

Raw signals are hashed and encrypted locally. The resulting Dynamic Environmental Signature is time-bound and single-use. Raw data never leaves the phone.

3

Proof sent to Misuto for verification

The signature is compared against your established environmental profile. Match means seamless access. Mismatch means blocked, instantly.

4

Access granted or denied in milliseconds

No push notification. No approval tap. No codes. The right person, in the right environment, gets in without friction. Everyone else is blocked.

FAQ

Enterprise questions, answered

How does Misuto align with compliance frameworks (NIST, SOC 2, GDPR)?

Misuto's zero-knowledge architecture is designed with regulatory compliance in mind. Environmental data is hashed on-device and never stored on our servers in raw form. We support NIST 800-63 AAL2+ requirements, SOC 2 Type II audit trails, and GDPR data minimization principles. Detailed compliance documentation is available during the demo process.

What's the typical deployment timeline?

Most enterprises complete initial deployment within 2-4 weeks. Phase 1 (shadow mode) runs Misuto alongside your existing MFA without enforcement, so you can validate environmental profiles. Phase 2 enables enforcement gradually by user group. No downtime, no migration windows.

What happens if an employee loses their phone?

IT can revoke the device's environmental profile immediately through the admin console. A replacement device auto-enrolls on first login with supervisor approval. Unlike hardware tokens, there's no logistics of shipping or retrieving physical devices.

How does Misuto handle remote and hybrid workers?

Employees can register multiple safe environments: home office, headquarters, co-working spaces. Each environment has its own signature profile with configurable time windows. Misuto adapts to how your workforce actually works, not just where your office is.

What if the verification server is unreachable?

Misuto fails closed by default. If verification cannot be completed, access is denied until connectivity is restored. For mission-critical applications, you can configure a time-limited cached verification with reduced scope. Policy is fully configurable per application.

Does Misuto replace our existing MFA?

Misuto sits in front of your IdP as a pre-authentication layer. It can replace traditional MFA (push notifications, OTP codes, hardware tokens) or run alongside it as an additional factor. Most enterprises phase out legacy MFA over 3-6 months as they gain confidence in environmental verification.

Can Misuto detect if someone is being coerced or under duress?

Misuto's environmental signatures include behavioral patterns (typical login times, usual locations, device proximity patterns). Significant deviations from an employee's normal pattern can trigger step-up verification or alert the security team, depending on your policy configuration.
